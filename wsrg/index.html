The Wireless Systems Research Group at Ohio University performs research in wireless networking, distributed systems, and mobile computing. We are part of the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in the Russ College of Engineering at OHIO.

Take a look at our research. If you are interested in working with us, please contact Prof. Harsha Chenji.
